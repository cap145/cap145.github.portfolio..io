Perfect Mask

Tools/software used: Photoshop

Project Reflection:

This project based on the artist John Baldessari and his artwork was a fun project, I throughly enjoyed creating the masks of these founds images. I think this art style is eyecatching and fun. In creating these artworks I chose found images that had a wide variety of contrast, which would help the bright colors shine through. I did not have any issues with this project, having used this style of masking in photoshop before, it was a project I could enjoy and really think about my photo choices.
